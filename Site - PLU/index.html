/About

Hey! I’m Mónica, a Graphic Designer since 2015

I’m 22 years old, Portuguese, travel addict and proud owner of a French Bulldog. Took my Bachelor’s in Graphic Design and Advertising in ESMAD, and I’m currently taking a Master in Interactive Media Sistems. 

I have worked as a Graphic Designer for both Oficina d’Imagem & M.Ferreira e Costa, while doing freelance work for many other clients. In April 2018, I founded A. (Anchor Point - Design Studio), where I could mainly explore my passion for Movies and Series throughout Design, and have been loving every minute of it! 

Got a challenge? I’m up for it!
